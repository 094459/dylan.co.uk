Routine and Gina Ford...

We have now established a fairly comfortable routine with Dylan, with perhaps small thanks to the Gina Ford Little Contented Baby book. This really has come as a godsend to us, and has given us some good ideas - we do not read too much into everything she says, as she seems to come across far to strict and rigid.

He now feeds at 7:00am, 10:00am, 2:00pm, 5:00pm, 7:00pm and then 11:00pm. He then has a cheeky midnight feast at around 2-3:00am (not that we are happy about that!), but we have told him our ideas about this....Weigh is still being gained, and he is becoming a little bit chubby much to our delight.

Dizzie Gillespie here Icome...

Smiles...
