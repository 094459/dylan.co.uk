Going to church....

After finally settling down into a semi routine, Dylan sleeping through the night and mum and dad finally finding some time to recoup - believe you me, you never know how good a full nights sleep is until you go without for a number of months!

After donning his specially tailored church suit (all the way from the spanish relies), we arrived early so we could feed him before mass. Unfortunately, it clashes with one of his feeds - however, the 10:30 mass we have been told is child friendly, and so we are looking forward to making lots of noise and causing a general disturbance.

Boyz in da hood.

Dylan striking a catalog pose

The day went really well. We met Father Kevin after mass (it had been a LONG time since mum and dad had entered the hallowed halls, and it was actually a not so bad experience - fully attended, with altar 'girls' and lots of singing and involvement) and went to the social club and were introduced to members of the local community.

The Weekend off - another visit to Lymington

We took Dylan to see Lolo and Lola again, as well as his aunts, uncles and cousins. This gave mum and dad the opportunity to sneak off for some quiet time in Christchurch harbour.

Errr, mum. What are you doing on my swing?

It had only been a few weeks since lolo and lola had seen Dylan, but he grows so much and so quickly. In May, he reached 15lb, and is above average for his corrected age - fantastic!
