First outing....

For aunty Trini's birthday, we decide to take Dylan on his first outing. This rapidly becomes a logistical exercise that would tax EXCEL, and we end up taking half of his stuff with us in Percy the Peugot and leaving little space for us. We are eagerly awaited, as Dylans cousins Mille and Sofia a waiting anxiously to play....oh dear, I hope they were not disappointed...

Dylan is strapped in and wondering where the steering wheel is...

Everyone is really excited to see Dylan, and we have a good but tiring time. Dylan spends all of his time (unsurprisingly) asleep....

Sofia, Grandpa Tony, Dylan and Millie...

Daddy, the girls and Dylan...

Healthcheck...

Dylan is doing really well by now, and is putting on weight fast. His head measurements have slowed down and are stable, and whilst he is still sickly, due to his reflux, this is not too worrying as he is gaining mass.

Most of February is spent trying to work a routine now that Dylan wakes once for a nightly feed. Dad is back at work (just) and commuting - it works out at 3.5 hours a day on trains (ouch!) but he does some work and seems to get a seat most days so is not too unhappy about the situation. Especially when he gets home to a nice house, and an even nicer family.

Restuarants and things....

Tanya and Martin visit, bring Anna, Tanya's mum and there new arrival Kathryn with them. We spend the night eating and drinking in a local restaurant, whilst Dylan sleeps in his pushchair - he doesn't seem to mind one bit, and enjoys the occasion attentions of the local waiting staff.
