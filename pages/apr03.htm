Busy Busy Busy

Its been a very busy month for Dylan, with good progress being made. Gina Ford is continuing her guru like status in the household as Dylan finally starts to get the hints that mama and papa would like to sleep throughout the night...

Dylan gets ready to rumble....

Dylan has become much more alert, and really looks at the world in total amazement. He has just realised how interesting his bedroom curtains are! He has had lots of presents recently, mainly clothes, and enjoys trying them on and strutting his stuff (or is that just mummy!)

You have got to let your body move to the music, strike a pose there is nothing to it....

Visitors...

We have lots of visitors this month, with mummies friends Jo and Ginny from up north in Yorkshire and Newcastle, making especially long journeys just to see Dylinski...

Oh please don't go....don't leave me with these mad people...

Ginny and Reg spent the night and took some fantastic photos of Dylan. They get some early practice for parenthood which lies just around the corner for them...

Coffee Morning....

Mummy holds a coffee morning during April. Lots of Dylan's friends turn up and he has a great time staring at them. Dylan already has his eye on this lovely young lady, Florence....Even three men come to the coffee morning which I am told is unusual for these events.

Florence and Dylan wondering where the rest of the magic roundabout crew are?

Walkies...

Mummy and Daddy take me on walks everywhere. I do enjoy them most of the time, but I tend to get very tired with so much to take in and have a quick power nap. It can get quite breezy walking by the sea, so we have to dress up warm.
